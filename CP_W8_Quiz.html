Cybersecurity - Overall Quiz

The five risk control strategies are:





Correct Answer: Defense, transference, mitigation, acceptance, and termination

Which risk control strategy is also referred to as Risk Avoidance in NIST SP 800-30?





Correct Answer: Termination

Which of the following is not true?





Correct Answer: Vernam cipher is implemented using tabula recta

Which of the following is NOT typically considered a factor affecting the cost of a control or safeguard?





Correct Answer: Marketing and advertising expenses

A website has an estimated value of $1 million. A hacker defacement scenario indicates that a deliberate act of sabotage could damage 10% of the site’s value. What is the Single Loss Expectancy (SLE)?





Correct Answer: $100,000

A website has a Single Loss Expectancy (SLE) of $100,000. If the Annualized Rate of Occurrence (ARO) is estimated at 0.50, what is the Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE)?





Correct Answer: $50,000

Which of the following is true regarding Cost-Benefit Analysis (CBA), Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE), and annualized cost of a safeguard (ACS)?





Correct Answer: CBA = ALE(prior) - ALE(post) - ACS
